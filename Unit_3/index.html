Ouray, CO, also known as the Switzerland of American, is a small mountain town located 5.5 hours east of Denver. Surrounded by the San Juan mountains, hotsprings and x river, there views are incredible. Unlike most of the mountain towns in Colorado, Ouray is not as devloped giving it a more western, country vibe.

Adventure awaits in Crested Butte! There is no shortage of things to do with hiking, mountain biking, fishing, rock-climbing, skiing, snowboarding, and so much more.

Colorado is famous for the Rocky Mountains and there is no better mountain town nestled in the Rockies other than Steamboat Springs!
